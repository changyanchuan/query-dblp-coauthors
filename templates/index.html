 Q: How to use this tool? 
 A: We need to obtain the pid of the author. 
     (1) Visit dblp.org and search the author who you would like to explore, such as Kaiming He. 
     (2) Visit Kaiming's author page on DBLP. The url of the his page is "https://dblp.org/pid/34/7659.html". 
     (3) The substring between pid and the html extension, i.e., '34/7659' is his pid on DBLP. 
     (3+) Pids can be in the following forms: 34/7659,  t/YufeiTao,  68/4942-1. 
  
 Q: Existing problems? 
 A: Listed as follows. 
     (1) A coauthor may have more than one pid. I didn't add any processes to solve these issues. Thus, using min_papers=1 is safer.
